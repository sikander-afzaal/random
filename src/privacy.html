1. Information You Provide to Us When Signing Up

When creating a Ribbon account without using your Facebook account, we collect your name, email address, phone number and a password. We use the term "User Basic Information" to describe the information you provide us about yourself at the time of sign up, whether or not through Facebook, and we use the term "User Friend Information" to describe the information you provide us about your friends at the time of sign up, whether or not through Facebook.

We use much of the User Basic Information to create your basic user profile and to communicate with you, mostly through email, and also to provide information to certain third parties as described in Section 7 of this Privacy Policy. We use your likes and interests collected from your Facebook account to improve your Ribbon experience by customizing the content and advertising seen by you while using the Service, communicating with you regarding information, content or features of the Service that are relevant to your interests or likes, or communicating with you to inform you of entertainment or commercial opportunities which may be of interest to you.

We use the User Friend Information to help create the Ribbon network that allows you to interact with other users of the Service and people who are not yet users of the Service.

Finally, we may use any of the User Basic Information or User Friend Information to provide customer support, back up our systems, allow for disaster recovery, and comply with certain legal obligations.
