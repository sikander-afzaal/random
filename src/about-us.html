About Us

Social planning is fragmented and broken. People use too many different applications to discover and plan events with friends.

People have limited visibility into friends event and travel plans. This problem results in missed experiences, missed connections, and inefficient planning.

Ribbon gives people the power to manage their entire social life in one place — making it easier and faster to plan events, experiences and travel with friends.
